A timeline of Boltzmann's Life:

  • 1844 - Born February 20, 1844, Vienna, Austria
  • 1863 - He studied physics and the University of Vienna.
  • 1866 - He received his PhD with a dissertation titled "The kinetic thoery of gases."
  • 1869 - He was appointed full Professor of Mathematical Physics at the University of Graz.
  • 1873 - Boltzmann joined the University of Vienna as Professor of Mathematics and there he stayed until 1876.
  • 1876 - Ludwig Boltzmann married Henriette von Aigentler, an apiring math and physics teacher.
  • 1890 - Boltzmann was appointed to the Chair of Theoretical Physics at the University of Munich in Bavaria, Germany.
